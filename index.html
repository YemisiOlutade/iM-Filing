automatically file DIRECTLY to the courts

Our goal with iM-filing is to save your team countless hours of manually submitting documents to the Nigerian courts in civil court cases. if you spend more than one-hour per day filing ALL pleadings at your firm, then you are wasting precious time.

iM-Filing is a product of Team impact that is not affilated with any law firm. We are a Nigeria ePortal Certified Third Party Vendor and specialize in automating the filing of court documents on existing cases with little to no human intervention.

We can reverse engineer your case management system to integrate it directly with the court and seamlessly file your document an save confirmation numbers, emails and date-stamped documents back to your system
